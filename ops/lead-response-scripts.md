## RESPONSE TIME SLA RULES

| Trigger | Action | Max time |
|---|---|---:|
| New lead (form/chat/WhatsApp) | SMS + attempt call | 5 min |
| No response | Follow-up SMS | 2 hours |
| Still no response | Final SMS | 24 hours |
| After job completion | Review request SMS | Same day |
| Post-job | Referral ask | 3-5 days |
| Missed call | Return call + SMS | 10 min |
