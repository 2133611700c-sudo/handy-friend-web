## FACEBOOK-SPECIFIC IMAGE TIPS

✅ **Size:** 1200x628px (optimal for FB feed)
✅ **Format:** PNG or JPG (both work)
✅ **Text overlay:** Max 20% of image (FB algorithm favors less text)
✅ **People:** Consider adding before/after with person pointing (increases engagement 20%)
✅ **Colors:** Bright, warm colors outperform dark colors 3:1

---

## NEXTDOOR-SPECIFIC IMAGE TIPS

✅ **Size:** 1200x628px recommended
✅ **Quality:** High resolution (no pixelated images)
✅ **Content:** Focus on LOCAL examples (neighborhood visible in background if possible)
✅ **Before/After:** CRITICAL for Nextdoor (people want proof)
✅ **Faces:** Avoid showing customer faces (respect privacy) UNLESS they consent

### ✅ DO:
