# Google Review — Setup & Templates

## Review Link Format
```
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID
```

**To get your Place ID:**
1. Go to https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/places/web-service/place-id-finder
2. Search "Handy & Friend Los Angeles"
3. Copy the Place ID
4. Replace YOUR_PLACE_ID above

**Short link option:** Create a shortlink via handyandfriend.com/review (already configured as redirect in vercel.json)

---

## When to Ask for Review

| Timing | Method | Template |
|---|---|---|
| Immediately after job | In-person ask | "We'd love your feedback — here's a quick link" |
| Same day, after leaving | SMS | Template A below |
| 2 days later (if no review) | WhatsApp | Template B below |

---

## Template A: Same-Day SMS
```
[NAME], thanks for choosing Handy & Friend!

If you have 30 seconds, a Google review helps other homeowners find us:
handyandfriend.com/review

Thank you!
— Handy & Friend
```

## Template B: 2-Day WhatsApp Follow-up
```
Hi [NAME]! Hope everything looks great with your [SERVICE].

If you're happy with the result, a quick Google review means a lot to our small business:
handyandfriend.com/review

We really appreciate it. Here if you need anything!
— Handy & Friend
```

## Template C: In-Person Script
```
"[NAME], I'm glad you're happy with the work. We're a small business
and reviews really help us. Would you mind leaving us a quick Google
review? I can text you the link right now — takes 30 seconds."
```

## Review Response Templates

### Positive Review (respond within 24h)
```
Thank you [NAME]! We loved working on your [SERVICE] project.
Looking forward to helping you again. — Handy & Friend team
```

### Negative Review (respond within 4h)
```
[NAME], thank you for your feedback. We take quality seriously
and want to make this right. Please call us at (213) 361-1700
so we can address your concern directly. — Handy & Friend
```

## KPI Target
- Ask rate: 100% of completed jobs
- Review rate: 50% of asks → review posted
- Target: 2-4 new reviews per month
- Response to all reviews: within 24h
