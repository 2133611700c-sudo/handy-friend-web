## PART 4: GOOGLE LOCAL SERVICES ADS (LSA)

### 📺 What is LSA?

**Google Local Services Ads = "Pay Per Lead" for Local Services**

- Shows at **very top of Google search** for "handyman near me"
- Shows **Google Guaranteed badge** (builds trust)
- Pay only when customer **calls or messages** you
- Perfect for: TV mounting, cabinet painting, furniture assembly, etc.
- Bad for: Products, online services

### **LSA: Cost Breakdown**

| Service | Cost Per Lead | Monthly Leads | Monthly Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| TV Mounting | $15-25 | 5-10 | $75-250 |
| Cabinet Painting | $20-30 | 3-8 | $60-240 |
| Interior Painting | $15-25 | 4-10 | $60-250 |
| Furniture Assembly | $12-20 | 3-7 | $36-140 |
| General Handyman | $18-28 | 8-15 | $144-420 |
| **TOTAL** | **$15-28 avg** | **20-40 leads** | **$300-800** |

### **LSA: Expected Results**

- **Leads/month:** 20-40 qualified leads
- **Close rate:** 25-35% (good for local services)
- **Jobs booked/month:** 5-14 jobs
- **Revenue/month:** $1,500-$7,000
- **ROI:** 200-500% (3-5x return on ad spend)

### LSA Setup Requirements:

- [ ] Google Local Services Ads account activated
- [ ] Service areas defined (LA neighborhoods)
- [ ] Customer reviews: 5+ (preferably 10+)
- [ ] Photos: 5+ work photos
- [ ] Response time: Must reply within 4 hours
- [ ] Business verification: Google may verify business
- [ ] Background check: May be required (varies by location)

### 🚨 LSA Key Rules:

1. **You only pay when someone contacts you** (calls, messages, or booking request)
2. **Google Guaranteed badge** = customer confidence
3. **Must respond fast** = Google tracks your response time
4. **Negative reviews hurt** = LSA visibility drops with bad reviews
5. **No refunds** = Once you're charged, that's it (but customer contacted you)

## PART 5: GOOGLE SEARCH ADS

### 📍 What is Search Ads?

**Google Search Ads = "Pay Per Click" on Google Search Results**

- Shows below LSA, in sponsored search results
- Pay each time someone **clicks** your ad (not when they contact you)
- You bid on keywords: "TV mounting LA", "cabinet painting near me", etc.
- Can work but higher cost than LSA for handyman

### **Search Ads: Cost Breakdown**

| Keyword | Avg CPC | Monthly Clicks | Monthly Cost | Est. Leads |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| "TV mounting LA" | $3-5 | 50-100 | $150-500 | 5-10 |
| "handyman Los Angeles" | $2-4 | 100-200 | $200-800 | 10-20 |
| "cabinet painting LA" | $3-6 | 40-80 | $120-480 | 4-8 |
| "furniture assembly" | $1-2 | 100-200 | $100-400 | 5-10 |
| **TOTAL** | **$2-4 avg** | **300-580 clicks** | **$200-500** | **15-30 leads** |

### **Search Ads: Expected Results**

- **Clicks/month:** 300-500 (varies by bid)
- **Conversion rate:** 5-10% (5-50 leads from clicks)
- **Cost per lead:** $10-25
- **Cost per job (at 30% close):** $33-83 per job
- **ROI:** 50-150% (lower than LSA)

### ⚠️ Search Ads Challenges for Handyman:
- Higher cost per lead than LSA
- More competition from larger companies
- "Click fraud" - some clicks won't convert
- Requires ongoing optimization

**Recommendation:** Focus on LSA first, add Search Ads if LSA is performing well.
