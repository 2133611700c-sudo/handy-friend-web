THE MODEL

$150 SERVICE CALL  (public price — standard small jobs)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  TV MOUNTING
    Included:    Standard wall mount up to 65" on drywall or studs.
                 Surface cable management included.
    Quote-only:  Hidden-wire / in-wall wiring. Fire-block walls.

  FURNITURE ASSEMBLY
    Included:    Small to medium pieces fitting in 2 hours.
                 IKEA shelves, desks, tables, small dressers.
    Quote-only:  PAX / Elfa closet systems. Bed frames. Multiple complex pieces.

  ART & MIRROR HANGING
    Included:    Up to 5 standard pieces, level-checked.
                 Drywall or stud walls.
    Quote-only:  Gallery walls over 5 pieces. Oversized mirrors with structural anchoring.

  PLUMBING (minor)
    Included:    Faucet swap. Shower head replacement. Toilet tank repair.
                 Re-caulk tub or shower.
    Quote-only:  New supply lines. Permit-required work. Anything beyond like-for-like.

  ELECTRICAL (minor)
    Included:    Light fixture replacement in existing box.
                 Outlet or switch swap. Smart doorbell / lock install.
    Quote-only:  New circuits. Panel work. Permit-required work.

  DRYWALL REPAIR
    Included:    Small patches up to 6 inches. Texture matching for same-color refresh.
    Quote-only:  Medium 6–12 in. Large 12+ in. Multi-coat color change. Water damage.

  DOOR REPAIR / ADJUSTMENT
    Included:    Hinge adjustment. Latch fix. Handle or knob replacement.
                 Strike plate alignment.
    Quote-only:  New door install. Pre-hung replacement. Exterior door install.

  Overflow: $75/hr after 2h — only when approved in writing before work continues.
