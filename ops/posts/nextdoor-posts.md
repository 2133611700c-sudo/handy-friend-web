**Title:** Quick tip before you mount that new TV

**Body:**
Got a new TV? Before you drill into the wall:

1. Use a stud finder — drywall anchors alone won't hold 50+ lbs safely
2. Measure twice — center of TV should be at eye level from your couch (usually 42-48 inches)
3. Plan your cables — hiding wires in-wall looks 10x cleaner
