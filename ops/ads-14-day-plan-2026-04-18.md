## Checkpoints & thresholds

### D+1 (2026-04-19) — Tracking sanity
**Check:**
- GA4 real-time shows `phone_click` events (any, not just from ads)
- Outbox still ok=true

**Threshold:** If `phone_click` event doesn't fire when clicking button → tracking broken, investigate shared.js/gtag.

### D+2 (2026-04-20) — Impression uplift
**Check:**
- Ads impressions (24h) ≥ 8 (baseline: ~5.8/day = 174/30)
- Campaign status still "Включено"

**Threshold:** <5 impressions = new assets not being served. Check if assets are "Допущено".

### D+3 (2026-04-21) — First ads lead
**Check:**
- `leads_by_source.google_ads_search` ≥ 1 in Supabase
- OR phone_click event fired with `page_referrer` matching google.com

**Threshold:** Still 0 after D+3 is expected; re-evaluate at D+7.

### D+5 (2026-04-23) — CTR improvement
**Check:**
- 7-day Ads CTR ≥ 4% (baseline 3.07%)
- Quality Score of top keywords trending up (check "Показатель качества" column)

**Threshold:** <3% = assets aren't helping. Review RSA relevance vs keyword intent.

### D+7 (2026-04-25) — Week 1 verdict
**Checks:**
- Weekly clicks ≥ 10 (baseline ~1.4/day, hoping 1.5x+)
- Weekly spend ≤ $45 (budget compliance)
- Lead count (any source) ≥ 5 (vs 4 this past week)
- Cost per click trending down (QS growing)

**Decision gate:**
- **Continue if:** leads ≥ 5 AND at least 1 attributable to ads
- **Fallback A if:** 0 leads from ads in 14 days total → narrow to single winner (TV mounting)
- **Fallback B if:** CPC > $5 stable → geo-narrow to Hollywood/WeHo/Silver Lake radius

### D+10 (2026-04-28) — Revenue visibility
**Check:**
- `jobs_completed` > 0 in 30-day stats
- Revenue > $0 indicating close rate not broken

**Threshold:** Still $0 with 5+ leads = sales funnel issue (not ads). Out of scope for ads plan.

- Total spend (14d): __ (max $89.60)

## Attribution notes

`google_ads_search` source tag in Supabase requires GCLID capture on lead creation. Current attribution may undercount ads contribution because:
- User clicks ad → arrives on site → opens chat → chat source labels "website_chat"
- Ad contribution is lost in last-click attribution

**D+7 improvement:** capture `gclid` URL param on landing → persist in sessionStorage → include on lead creation for proper attribution.
