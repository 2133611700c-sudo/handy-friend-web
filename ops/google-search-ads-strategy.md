## SETUP PROCESS

### Step 1: Create Search Ads Campaign

1. Go to: `google.com/ads`
2. Click "Campaigns" (left menu)
3. Click "+ Campaign"
4. Select: "Search"
5. Campaign goal: "Leads" or "Sales"
6. Campaign name: "HF - TV Mounting" (or your campaign)

### Step 2: Set Budget & Bidding

1. **Daily budget:** $3-5/day (($90-150/month)
2. **Bidding strategy:** "Maximize clicks" (start here, then optimize)
3. **Geographic targets:** Los Angeles, CA (and expand to surrounding areas if needed)
4. **Language:** English

### Step 3: Create Ad Groups

1. Click "+ Ad group"
2. Ad group name: "TV Mounting Exact"
3. Add keywords: [TV mounting Los Angeles], [TV mount installation], etc.
4. Set bid for group: $4-6 (for exact match)

### Step 4: Write Ads

1. Click "Ads & Extensions"
2. Click "+ Ad"
3. Choose: "Responsive Search Ad" (recommended)
4. Add headlines (up to 15)
5. Add descriptions (up to 4)
6. Add final URL: handyandfriend.com
7. Click "Save"

### Step 5: Add Extensions

**Call extension:**
1. Click "Extensions"
2. Click "+ Extension"
3. Select "Call"
4. Phone number: (213) 361-1700
5. Save

**Location extension:**
1. Click "Extensions"
2. Select "Location"
3. Choose your business location
4. Save

**Promotion extension:**
1. Text: "Save 20% with combo offer"
2. Start/end dates
3. Promotion code: COMBO20

## CONVERSION TRACKING (Critical!)

### Setup Google Conversion Tracking

**Why:** Know which keywords = customers

1. **Go to:** Google Ads > Tools & Settings > Conversions
2. **Click:** "+ Conversion"
3. **Select:** "Website"
4. **Conversion type:** "Purchase" or "Contact"
5. **Fill in:**
   - Name: "Contact Form Submission"
   - Value: $500 (average job)
   - Category: "Lead"

6. **Get conversion code:**
   - Google provides JavaScript code
   - Give to developer to add to handyandfriend.com form

7. **Track phone calls:**
   - Google provides phone tracking number
   - Replace main number with Google tracking number in ads
   - Or use call extension (tracks automatically)

## DAILY OPERATIONS

### Monitoring Dashboard:

**Check daily (takes 5 min):**
- [ ] Impressions: How many people saw your ads?
- [ ] Clicks: How many clicked your ad?
- [ ] Click-through rate (CTR): % of viewers who clicked
- [ ] Cost per click (CPC): How much each click costs
- [ ] Conversions: How many became leads?
- [ ] Cost per conversion: How much per lead?

**Target metrics:**
- CTR: 2-5% (handyman ads)
- CPC: $2-4 avg
- Conversion rate: 5-10% (2-5 leads per 100 clicks)
- Cost per lead: $10-25

### Weekly Optimization:

**Check weekly:**
- [ ] Which keywords are converting? (bid up)
- [ ] Which keywords are wasting money? (pause or lower bid)
- [ ] Which ads have best CTR? (use for future ads)
- [ ] Is budget spent evenly? (adjust bids)

**Make changes:**
- Pause low-performing keywords
- Increase bids on top performers
- Test new ad copy
- Adjust daily budget if needed

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: No conversions even with clicks

**Causes:**
- Conversion tracking not set up properly
- Landing page not good (people click but leave)
- No phone answer (people don't get through)
- Price too high (people see price, don't call)

**Fix:**
- Verify conversion pixel is installed on website
- Check handyandfriend.com/contact form works
- Make sure phone number works & answered quickly
- Add pricing in ad copy if possible

### Problem: Cost per lead too high ($30+)

**Causes:**
- Bids too high (reduce bids)
- Wrong keywords (people searching "DIY" not "professional")
- Low quality score (Google penalizes)
- Budget too low (can't compete for top position)

**Fix:**
- Lower bids on low-converting keywords
- Pause "DIY" related keywords
- Improve ad quality (better headlines, descriptions)
- Remove irrelevant keywords
- Make sure landing page is relevant

### Problem: Very few impressions

**Causes:**
- Bids too low (not showing in top spots)
- Budget too low (ads stop running midday)
- Keyword not popular
- Geographic targeting too narrow

**Fix:**
- Raise bids on high-intent keywords
- Increase daily budget
- Add more keywords (with lower bids)
- Expand geographic targeting
