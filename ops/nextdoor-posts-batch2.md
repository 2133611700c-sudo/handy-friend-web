## POST ND-20260304-06: Echo Park — Interior Painting (Landlord-Friendly Neutrals)

**Title:** Painting your Echo Park rental? The colors that actually get your deposit back

**Body:**
If you're a renter in Echo Park and want to repaint, the safest move is to use the same sheen level as what was there before — and stick to warm whites or light greiges rather than pure white, which almost always looks different from original builder paint.

Most landlords are fine with repaints as long as you return walls to a neutral and match the finish (eggshell is the most common for interiors). Sherwin-Williams Accessible Beige and Agreeable Gray are two that almost never cause disputes at move-out.

We do interior painting from $3/sq ft labor-only (you choose the paint, or we can source it). A typical 12x12 bedroom runs $108-$130 in labor.

If you're not sure whether your landlord will approve, we can help you frame the request — most say yes when they see it's a professional, insured job.
