## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: LSA not showing up in search results

**Causes:**
- Business not yet verified (wait 1-3 days)
- GBP not linked to ads account
- Service area not set
- No customer reviews yet

**Fix:**
- Verify GBP is connected to ads account
- Check service areas include your target neighborhoods
- Request reviews (minimum 3-5 required)
- Wait 24-48 hours

### Problem: "Not approved" message

**Common reasons:**
- Missing business verification
- Background check not completed
- Service license required (CA may require)
- Profile info incomplete

**Fix:**
- Check email from Google for specific reason
- Click "Request review" or "Fix this"
- Complete requested information
- Resubmit

### Problem: Getting very few leads

**Causes:**
- LSA newly launched (needs 3-5 days to gain traction)
- Budget too low ($20/day may be limiting)
- Bad reviews pulling down rank
- Service area too narrow

**Fix:**
- Wait 1 week before optimizing
- Raise budget to $25-30/day
- Request reviews to improve rating
- Expand service area to more neighborhoods

### Problem: Leads but no callbacks from you

**Critical:** Google tracks this!
- LSA ranking drops if you don't respond
- May be disapproved if response time is consistently slow

**Fix:**
- Set phone notifications for Google Ads leads
- Check dashboard every 2-3 hours minimum
- Respond to all leads within 15 min if possible
- If too many leads → Lower budget
