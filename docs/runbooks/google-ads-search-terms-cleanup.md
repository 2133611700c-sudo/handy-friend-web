# Google Ads Search Terms Cleanup Runbook

Status: ACTIVE

## Goal

Stop wasting budget on dirty queries and make paid traffic measurable.

## Weekly workflow

1. Export Search terms for last 7 and 30 days.
2. Group by service intent.
3. Add strong negatives for irrelevant terms.
4. Promote proven high-intent terms to exact or phrase match.
5. Compare Ads conversions with GA4 and CRM proof.

## Negative keyword buckets

- jobs / hiring / career / salary
- DIY / how to / tutorial / YouTube
- free / cheap / low cost where unqualified
- directories and franchise noise
- unrelated brands and marketplaces
- wholesale / supplier / parts-only queries

## Keep candidates

- tv mounting near me
- handyman near me
- furniture assembly near me
- drywall repair near me
- cabinet painting near me
- interior painting handyman

## Stop rule

Do not scale spend while Ads conversions are zero or CRM proof does not match paid clicks.
