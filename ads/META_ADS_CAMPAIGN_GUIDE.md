## **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **Low CTR? (<1%)**
- Revise ad copy (make more urgent)
- Change images (use professional photos)
- Test new CTA (try "Book Now" vs "Get Quote")
- Check mobile formatting

### **High CPC? (>$1)**
- Narrow audience (age, interests)
- Increase daily budget (get better auction placement)
- Improve landing page quality (Meta favors fast, mobile-friendly pages)
- Add negative keywords (exclude irrelevant searches)

### **Low Conversion Rate? (<2%)**
- Check landing page (is lead form above fold?)
- Simplify form (reduce fields)
- Add trust signals (reviews, guarantee, phone number)
- Test different ad-to-landing-page combos
