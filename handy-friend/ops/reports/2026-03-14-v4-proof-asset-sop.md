# V4 Proof Asset SOP — Cabinet Painting Priority
Date: 2026-03-14
Owner: Sergii

## 1) Purpose
Create reliable trust assets for conversion and local ranking support.
No fake proof. No stock-image substitution.

## 2) Minimum Capture Per Completed Cabinet Job
- 3 before photos
- 3 after photos
- 1 wide kitchen shot
- 1 close-up finish shot
- 1 short process video (10-20 sec)
- 1 client quote/testimonial text

## 3) Capture Checklist (On Site)
1. Before photos taken before prep starts.
2. Process clip during prep/spray/final pass.
3. After photos taken in same angles as before.
4. Save neighborhood + date + service notes.
5. Ask for testimonial sentence before leaving.

## 4) Naming Convention
Format: `YYYY-MM-DD_service_area_stage_seq.ext`
Examples:
- `2026-03-14_cabinet-hollywood_before_01.jpg`
- `2026-03-14_cabinet-hollywood_after_01.jpg`
- `2026-03-14_cabinet-hollywood_process_01.mp4`

## 5) Storage Contract
- Raw incoming: `artifacts/imports/`
- Published exports: `artifacts/exports/`
- Website-ready service assets: target site assets folder [Needs Verification path]

## 6) Publishing Matrix
| Channel | Asset package | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| `/cabinet-painting` page | Best 1 BA pair + caption + date + area | Every completed cabinet job |
| GBP | 3-5 photos + short result caption | Weekly |
| Facebook/Nextdoor | 1 BA pair + CTA | 2-3 posts/week |
| Reviews page | Quote + linked service mention | Weekly update |

## 7) Caption Template
`Kitchen cabinet painting completed in {{area}}. Scope: {{scope}}. Timeline: {{days}} days. Result: {{result}}.`

## 8) Quality Gates
- Before/after are different files and different timestamps.
- No watermark, no AI-only proof, no duplicate placeholder assets.
- At least one text proof linked to each new BA set.

## 9) Failure Handling
If no real completed-job assets this week:
- Do NOT publish fabricated BA content.
- Publish neutral educational/offer post only.
- Mark gap in weekly report and prioritize asset capture next job.

## 10) Weekly Audit
Every Friday verify:
- Number of new BA sets captured.
- Number published to site.
- Number published to GBP.
- Number of review asks sent.
