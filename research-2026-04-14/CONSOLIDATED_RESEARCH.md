## ⚠ CRITICAL FINDINGS (immediate action required)

### 🔴 LEGAL RISK — CSLB license compliance
**Source:** ChatGPT, Sub-agent D
- Site uses "Licensed and insured professionals" on furniture/plumbing/electrical pages
- CSLB minor-work exemption: only valid for jobs < $500 total labor + materials, AND cannot advertise as "licensed contractor" without actual license
- **Risk:** Regulatory complaint, GMB suspension, loss of trust
- **Fix:** Remove "Licensed" wording. Replace with "Insured · Background-checked · Small jobs welcome"
- **Safe categories for GMB:** Handyman (primary), Furniture assembly, Picture framing. Avoid: Electrician (C-10), Plumber (C-36), GC (B), Flooring (C-15)

### 🔴 PUBLIC ADDRESS EXPOSURE
**Source:** ChatGPT
- 1213 Gordon St visible on site publicly
- For service-area business, Google allows hiding address
- **Fix:** Remove street address from visible service pages; keep in schema only
