## PART 11: LAUNCHING THE PAGE

### Step-by-Step Facebook Page Creation

1. **Log into Facebook.com**
2. Click **Pages** (left sidebar)
3. Click **Create New Page**
4. Choose:
   - Page name: `Handy & Friend`
   - Category: `Handyman Service` (primary)
5. Add additional categories: Home Repair Service, Home Improvement Service
6. Click **Create Page**

7. **Upload Profile Picture**
   - Click profile picture area → Upload photo
   - Use logo or branded image
   - Size: 320x320px minimum

8. **Upload Cover Image**
   - Click cover image area → Upload photo
   - Size: 1200x628px
   - Use template from **PART 3**

9. **Add Basic Information**
   - Click **About** → **Edit**
   - Paste the **About Section** text from **PART 1** (copy exactly)
   - Add phone number, website, hours, service areas

10. **Add Call-to-Action Buttons**
    - Settings → **Edit Page Info** → Buttons
    - Primary: "Call Now" (213-361-1700)
    - Secondary: "Send Message"

11. **Enable Messenger & Auto-Reply**
    - Settings → **Messaging**
    - Follow steps in **PART 7**

12. **Create Username**
    - Settings → **Username**
    - Wait until you have 25+ followers
    - Choose from options in **PART 6**

13. **Publish First 3 Posts**
    - Copy **POST 1**, **POST 2**, **POST 3** from **PART 5**
    - Post them (don't schedule yet, post immediately)
    - Pin POST 1 and POST 2

14. **Schedule Remaining Posts**
    - Copy **POST 4-10** from **PART 5**
    - Schedule for following days using Facebook Scheduling tool
